Add an **Add Job** button at the top so recruiters can create a new job posting.

When the recruiter clicks **Candidates** on a job, open the **Candidate Data Pool** page for that job. Show the recruiter header as **John Recruiter**, a **Run AI Screening** button, search input, **Select All**, selected count, and total candidates in pool. Display candidate cards/rows such as **Manual Candidate 1**, labeled manually added, skill **Communication – Intermediate**, location **Unknown**, experience **2.3 years**, and **Kalisa Aime**, role **Senior Backend Engineer**, skill **Node.js – Expert**, location **Kigali, Rwanda**. After selecting candidates and clicking **Run AI Screening**, send the selected job and candidate data to the AI screening flow. The result should appear in the **AI Shortlists** page. This page should show summary cards: **Total Screened**, **Shortlisted**, **Avg AI Score**, and **Incomplete CVs**. Also show a warning/info section for incomplete or poorly formatted resumes, with skipped candidates requiring manual review. Add filters/buttons for **Top 10**, **Top 20**, **Top 30**, and **Refresh AI Screening Results**.

On the **AI Shortlists** page, replace **Export JSON** with **Export Excel**. The export button should download the shortlist results as an `.xlsx` file, including candidate name, role/job, AI score, ranking, strengths, gaps, relevance notes, location, experience, and screening status. The shortlisted candidates table should show ranked candidates and allow clicking a row to view full AI reasoning.

The **AI Settings** page should include recruiter settings for the AI screening engine. Add a **Primary AI Model** dropdown with Gemini model options such as **gemini-2.5-pro** and **gemini-2.5-flash-lite**. Add pipeline toggles for **Multi-candidate Batch Output**, **Explainable Structuring**, and **Bias Detection Filter**.
